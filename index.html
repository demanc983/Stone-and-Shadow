It’s Arlo’s birthday. Can he and his brother Axel find the present hidden by Mamma and Pappa?

How to Play

How to play

You control Axel and Arlo together — one brother leads and the other follows. Tap SWITCH to take control of the other brother.
Use the on-screen pad (with diagonals) to move, ACT to read/open/dig/talk/pet, POWER for your amulet, and SWITCH to swap brother.
Arlo can slip into shadow. Axel is strong as stone.
